Description

A lasagna is a hearty Italian dish made of layered pasta sheets, rich meat or vegetable sauce, creamy cheese, and baked until bubbling and golden.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef or Italian sausage
  • Tomato sauce or crushed tomatoes
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Eggs (optional, for ricotta mixture)
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and black pepper
  • Italian seasoning or dried basil & oregano

How to Cook Lasagna

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions.
  3. In a pan, sauté onion and garlic, then cook the ground beef or sausage until browned.
  4. Add tomato sauce and seasonings to the meat; simmer for 10 minutes.
  5. Mix ricotta cheese with an egg (optional) in a bowl.
  6. In a baking dish, layer noodles, meat sauce, ricotta mixture, and mozzarella cheese.
  7. Repeat layers until all ingredients are used, finishing with mozzarella and Parmesan on top.
  8. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes, then remove foil and bake another 20 minutes until golden and bubbly.
  9. Let the lasagna rest for 10 minutes before serving.
